مانجو مجمدة (IQF Mango)

We offer you premium frozen mangoes, selected from the finest Egyptian mango farms and frozen using IQF technology to preserve their natural taste, perfect texture, and rich golden color.

Product Features:

  • Rich flavor and natural sugar content.
  • Permanent golden color after melting, with no change in color or taste.
  • Completely free of any additives or preservatives.

Product Specifications:

  • Available shapes: 10x10 mm or 20x20 mm slices or cubes (as per customer request)
  • Varieties: Zabidiya – Naomi – Kate (as per customer request)
  • Packaging: 800 g / 1 kg / 2.5 kg / 10 kg bags (bulk)
  • Shelf life: 18 months at -18°C or cooler.
  • Country of origin: 🇪🇬 Egypt
